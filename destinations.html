Destinations

Seven countries. One shortlist designed around you.

Every country below has a dedicated admissions guide with fees, deadlines, visa rules, top universities and scholarship tips specific to UAE and GCC students.

Book destination advice →
🇬🇧

United Kingdom

Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, Edinburgh. Three-year undergrads, Graduate Route visa, Clarendon & Rhodes scholarships, and the densest Master's market in Europe.

  • QS Top-100 programmes: 70+
  • Visa: Graduate Route (2–3 yrs)
  • Intakes: Sept / Jan
UK admissions guide →
🇺🇸

United States

MIT, Harvard, Stanford and 4,000 institutions. Need-blind aid at elite privates, deep STEM pipelines, and the longest post-study-work runway via OPT + STEM-OPT.

  • QS Top-100 programmes: 150+
  • Visa: F-1 + OPT (1–3 yrs)
  • Intakes: Fall / Spring
USA admissions guide →
🇨🇦

Canada

Toronto, UBC, McGill, Waterloo. Three-year PGWP, clear PR pathways, and a welcoming immigration stance that other countries have quietly lost.

  • QS Top-100 programmes: 30+
  • Visa: PGWP (up to 3 yrs)
  • Intakes: Sept / Jan / May
Canada admissions guide →
🇦🇺

Australia

Melbourne, UNSW, ANU, Monash. Two intakes a year, a lifestyle you can live in, and a post-study work visa (subclass 485) of 2 to 4 years.

  • QS Top-100 programmes: 50+
  • Visa: 485 (2–4 yrs)
  • Intakes: Feb / July
Australia admissions guide →
🇦🇪

United Arab Emirates

Khalifa, NYU Abu Dhabi, American University of Sharjah, Heriot-Watt Dubai. World-class campuses in your backyard — often with substantial scholarship aid.

  • World-ranked universities: 10+
  • Residence visa via institution
  • Intakes: Sept / Jan
UAE admissions guide →
🇲🇾

Malaysia

University of Malaya, Monash Malaysia, Nottingham Malaysia, Taylor's. World-ranked teaching at roughly a third of UK or Australian cost — with twin-degree routes.

  • Ranked universities: 15+
  • Post-study employment pass
  • Intakes: Jan / May / Sept
Malaysia admissions guide →
🇸🇬

Singapore

NUS, NTU. Asia's innovation capital, tightly government-funded, with global finance & tech employers quite literally at the gate.

  • QS Top-20: NUS (#8), NTU (#15)
  • Visa: 12-month LTVP post-study
  • Intakes: Aug
