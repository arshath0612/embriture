Application process

Your path to Australia, in six moves.

Shortlist programmes and scholarships in parallel

Australia publishes scholarship amounts up front — so we shortlist programmes and funding simultaneously, not sequentially.

Take IELTS or PTE Academic

IELTS 6.5 overall (no band below 6.0) is the common benchmark. PTE Academic is widely accepted and often quicker to score.

Apply direct to universities

Most Australian universities accept direct online applications. No central UCAS-equivalent. Deadlines are typically 2–3 months before each intake.

Accept offer, pay deposit, get CoE

Once you accept, university issues a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE). Deposit is usually 1 semester's tuition.

Subclass 500 Student Visa

Apply online via ImmiAccount. Requires GTE statement, OSHC health cover, financial evidence, CoE. Typical processing: 4–8 weeks.

Pre-departure & arrival

OSHC activation, accommodation, Medicare-equivalent cover, opening bank account (Commonwealth, ANZ, Westpac open student accounts online pre-arrival).

Visa & post-study work

The Australia visa route in plain English.

Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Study

Subclass 500 Student Visa. Valid for duration of studies. Work up to 48 hours per fortnight during term, full-time in vacations. Family members can join as dependants.

Graduate Visa (Subclass 485)

Post-Study

Subclass 485 (Temporary Graduate Visa). Post-Study Work stream: 2 years (Bachelor's/Master's by coursework), 3 years (Master's by research), 4 years (PhD). Extensions available for regional study.

Skills in Demand Visa

Sponsorship

Skills in Demand Visa. Replaced TSS in late 2024. Needs employer sponsorship and alignment to skills occupation list.

Permanent Residency

PR Pathways

Permanent Residency. Skilled Independent (189), Skilled Nominated (190) and employer-sponsored 186 are main PR paths. Points-based — Australian study gives bonus points.

Living & costs

What it actually costs to live here.

Monthly Budget

Finance

Monthly budget. Sydney/Melbourne AUD 1,800–2,700; Brisbane/Perth AUD 1,400–2,000; Adelaide/Canberra AUD 1,300–1,800.

Accommodation

Housing

Accommodation. University halls or purpose-built student housing (Urbanest, Scape, Iglu) — typically AUD 280–450/week. Shared apartments cheaper at AUD 180–320/week.

Healthcare

OSHC

Healthcare. Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) is mandatory for the full duration of your visa. Typical cost AUD 550/year for singles.

Transport

Transit

Transport. Most cities are car-dependent once you're out of the centre. Melbourne and Sydney have usable public transport. Student concession varies by state (NSW is restrictive; Victoria is generous).

Scholarships & funding

The funded routes into Australia.

Australia Awards

Gov Award

Australia Awards. Australia's flagship development scholarship for eligible countries (UAE not currently on the list; India is). Full tuition, living allowance, flights.

Group of Eight Awards

Go8 Unis

Group of Eight university scholarships. Melbourne Intl Undergraduate, UNSW Intl Scholarship, ANU Chancellor's, Sydney Intl Student Award — all offer partial tuition reductions of 10–50% for strong academic profiles.

Research Training (RTP)

PhD Research

Research Training Program (RTP). Full PhD funding — tuition + stipend — at all Go8 universities. Competitive; apply via each university's graduate research office.

Destination Australia

Regional

Destination Australia. AUD 15,000/year for students at regional campuses. Combines well with university scholarships to bring total cost significantly down.
