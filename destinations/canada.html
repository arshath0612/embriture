Application process

Your path to Canada, in six moves.

Shortlist universities and programmes

Canada weighs academic grades and programme fit heavily. We build a mix of stretch (Toronto, McGill, UBC), fit (Waterloo, Western, McMaster, Queen's) and safety options.

Take IELTS / TOEFL

Most require IELTS 6.5 overall (no band below 6.0). Some French-language McGill programmes accept TEF/TEFAQ.

Apply via OUAC (Ontario) or direct

Ontario UG: OUAC by mid-January. Other provinces: direct to each university. PG: direct, deadlines from December (top schools) to March.

SOP, LORs and portfolio

Personal essays are shorter than US apps but carry weight. 2–3 academic/professional recommendations. Portfolio/research proposal for design & PhD programmes.

Receive offer + GIC + proof of funds

Canadian study permits require a GIC (CAD 20,635 for 2026) plus proof of tuition paid or funds for first year.

Study Permit application

Online via IRCC. Biometrics at VFS Dubai. Typical processing: 6–10 weeks. SDS (Student Direct Stream) available to UAE students with qualifying profiles — processing as quick as 20 days.

Visa & post-study work

The Canada visa route in plain English.

Study Permit

Study

Study Permit. Valid for duration of studies plus 90 days. Work up to 24 hours/week during studies (20 pre-2024 rule revised). Full-time in vacations.

PGWP Work Permit

Post-Study

PGWP (Post-Graduation Work Permit). Open work permit for 1–3 years based on programme length. A 2-year Master's → 3-year PGWP; a 1-year Master's → 1-year PGWP. Only available for eligible PGWP-approved institutions.

Express Entry & PR

Settlement

Express Entry & PR. After 1+ year of Canadian work experience, most graduates qualify for PR through Canadian Experience Class via Express Entry. Points for age, education, English, work experience — Master's adds significant points.

Spousal Open Work Permit

Family Visa

Spouse Open Work Permit. Spouses of Master's and PhD students get an open work permit. As of 2024, spouses of most UG students no longer qualify — check current rules before committing.

Living & costs

What it actually costs to live here.

Monthly Budget

Finance

Monthly budget. Toronto/Vancouver CAD 1,800–2,800; Montreal/Ottawa CAD 1,400–2,000; smaller cities CAD 1,200–1,600.

Accommodation

Housing

Accommodation. First-year UG often in residence (CAD 11,000–15,000/year incl. meal plan). Off-campus shared apartments are the norm after year one.

Healthcare

Insurance

Healthcare. Mandatory provincial or university-provided insurance. BC's MSP, Ontario's UHIP and Alberta's AHCIP cover the basics.

Winter Realities

Climate

Winter realities. Winters are real. Budget CAD 400–800 for a proper winter jacket, boots and layers. Toronto and Vancouver are milder than the prairies.

Scholarships & funding

The funded routes into Canada.

Lester B. Pearson (UofT)

Full Ride

Lester B. Pearson (UofT). Canada's flagship full-ride international UG scholarship. Covers tuition, books, residence, meals. Extremely competitive — principal nomination from school required.

McCall MacBain (McGill)

Leadership

McCall MacBain (McGill). Full-funding Master's scholarship at McGill for applicants with leadership impact.

Entrance Scholarships

Automatic

Entrance Scholarships. Virtually every Canadian university offers automatic entrance awards — CAD 5,000–30,000 — based on school grades. We identify the ones you qualify for and how to stack them.

Vanier Scholarships

Doctoral

Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships. CAD 50,000/year for 3 years for doctoral students. Combined with university funding often covers the entire PhD.
