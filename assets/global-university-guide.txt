🇬🇧  UNITED KINGDOM (UK)
The UK hosts some of the world's oldest and most prestigious universities. Oxford and Cambridge rank in the global top 5. Undergraduate programmes typically take 3 years; postgraduate taught degrees 1 year. UK degrees are globally recognised, with a 2-year Graduate Route visa available post-graduation. English-language instruction and a vibrant multicultural environment make the UK a top destination.
