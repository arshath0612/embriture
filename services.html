How pricing works

Transparent. Capped. No commission traps.

Every service is priced up front in your engagement letter. We don't take commissions from universities — which means our shortlist is for you, not for whoever is paying the highest finder's fee this quarter. Packages range from AED 3,000 (single-country Master's) to AED 25,000 (multi-country, full undergraduate & scholarship mentorship). We also offer interest-free instalments for UAE residents.
