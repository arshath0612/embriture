Services · PhD Mentorship

A research match, not just an admission.

PhD admissions are a supervisor-matching game dressed up as an application. We help you find the right lab, craft a credible research proposal and apply for funding — not just tick boxes.

Book free consultation →See the process
Who it's for

Built for Master's graduates aiming at PhD admissions.

Applicants to UK, USA, Canada, UAE and Singapore doctoral programmes across engineering, computer science, medicine, humanities, social sciences and basic sciences. Supports Master's-by-research applications too.

What we work on

The research-first approach.

Research interest refinement, literature-review support, methodology framing, proposal drafting, CV tailoring, supervisor identification, cold-outreach drafting, interview prep, and full-funding applications.

Supervisor matching

Your PhD stands or falls on your supervisor.

We run systematic searches across recent publications, find supervisors whose active research aligns with yours, analyse their group size and publication rhythm, and help you draft targeted outreach emails. No generic templates.

Fully-funded routes

Gates, Clarendon, Vanier, CSC, Khalifa, A*STAR.

Most of our PhD applicants aim for fully-funded positions. We map the major scholarship routes and align your application timeline to each.

Interview & viva prep

Research defences with current PhDs.

We pair you with a current or recent PhD from a comparable programme for a live research-defence rehearsal. Much more valuable than a generic mock interview.

How it works

Our PhD Mentorship process.

Research direction consultation

A 90-minute session to map your research interests against 3–5 viable doctoral directions.

Literature scan + supervisor shortlist

We identify 10–20 candidate supervisors across target countries; you review, narrow, and we help draft outreach.

Research proposal drafting

3–5 drafts of a 2,000–5,000 word proposal through a senior research mentor.

Cold outreach + supervisor calls

We help you secure supervisor conversations before submitting applications — often decisive.

Application + funding bundle

Parallel applications to programme + funding (Clarendon, Gates, Vanier, Khalifa, A*STAR etc.). One submission calendar across all.

Interview & viva defence prep

Live research defence rehearsals with a current PhD in a similar field.
