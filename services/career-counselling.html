Services · Career Counselling

Plan the career. Then choose the course.

We start every family with a career thesis — a ten-year written plan of who you could become, supported by psychometric data, current labour-market research and your own constraints.

Book free consultation →See the process
Who it's for

Built for Year-9 through working professionals.

Career Counselling is our foundational service. It's for students still deciding subjects, parents trying to pressure-test a path, and working professionals considering a pivot. Every other service we offer plugs into the thesis this one produces.

What you get

A written career thesis, not a sales pitch.

By the end of our engagement you have a 12-to-20-page document covering your aptitude profile, 3–5 realistic career paths, the degrees and certifications that lead there, scholarship overlap, and a 12-month action plan.

12–20 Page written career thesis
3–5 Realistic career paths identified
2–3 Deep 90-minute sessions
Psychometric tools we use

Real science, not buzzfeed quizzes.

We run a combination of the Strong Interest Inventory, the Morrisby assessment, abstract reasoning tests and structured aptitude interviews. Every test is administered by a trained counsellor, not a self-serve platform.

Labour-market modelling

Where hiring is actually growing.

We pull live data from LinkedIn Economic Graph, UK government SOC codes, US BLS projections and GCC ministry labour data so your thesis is based on 2026 reality, not 2015 WhatsApp forwards.

Parent-student alignment

The conversation that everyone needs.

Many first consultations are really three — us, the student, and the parent — separately and together. A career thesis that only the student agrees with is a thesis that won't survive Year 13.

How it works

Our Career Counselling process.

Initial intake call (free)

30 minutes to understand the family, the student, and the actual questions. No commitment.

Psychometric battery

Strong Interest Inventory + Morrisby + structured aptitude interview, administered over 2 sessions.

Labour-market research

Your lead counsellor pulls live hiring, salary and geographic data for the career paths that fit your profile.

Draft thesis + family review

We share a 12–20 page draft, then workshop it with student and parents together.

Final thesis + 12-month action plan

Clean final document with specific, dated next steps — tests to take, subjects to choose, extracurriculars to pursue.
