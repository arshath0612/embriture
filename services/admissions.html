Services · Admissions Mentorship

Senior editors. Honest drafts. Real offers.

We don't write your application — we coach you to write a better one than you thought you could. Every SOP, LOR, personal statement and essay is reviewed by a senior editor who's read thousands of them.

Any application bound for the 35+ universities we partner with. We work best with students who are willing to write and rewrite — we're editors, not ghostwriters.

How it works

Our Admissions Mentorship process.

Intake + profile review

We read every transcript, test score and extracurricular document you have. Output: a candid competitiveness read against each university on your shortlist.

Story architecture

Before anyone writes a word, we build your application narrative — what are we trying to say, what evidence supports it, and what should go where.

Draft → senior-edit cycles

You draft. A senior editor edits with comments, not rewrites. You revise. Repeat 3–5 times per essay until both sides feel it's ready.

LOR briefings

We prepare short briefs for each of your recommenders so their letters reinforce — not contradict — your application narrative.

Submission review

Two-person sign-off on every submission. One primary counsellor, one second-reader.

Interview prep + offer decisions

Mock interviews with alumni. Once offers land, we run the full-cost comparison and help you accept the right one.
