Services · Visa & Pre-departure

The boring, critical part — done right.

A missed CAS, a wrongly-filed I-20 or a weak GTE statement can sink an offer you spent a year earning. We don't let that happen.

Book free consultation →See the process
Who it's for

Built for Students holding a valid offer letter from any university globally.

Whether you did your applications through eMBriture or applied independently, our visa support desk is open to all students requiring a high-probability application for the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, UAE, Malaysia or Singapore.

Complete assembly

Complete file assembly.

We assemble every required document, format bank statements and source-of-wealth declarations properly, translate non-English documents, and run pre-submission checks.

Financial audit

Financial profiling & source audit.

More than half of visa rejections are due to "insufficient funds" or "unclear source of wealth". We audit your parents' or sponsors' bank profiles to ensure they meet exact consular rules.

Interviews

Mock interview prep.

For countries requiring live or video interviews (USA, and sometimes UK or Canada), we run 1-on-1 mock interviews covering consular questions and body language.

Recovery

Prior refusal recovery.

If you have a prior rejection, we request your consular notes (e.g. GCMS notes for Canada), draft a fresh statement of purpose addressing the refusal point, and re-apply.

01 / What we cover

From sticker to stamp.

CAS/I-20/CoE review, financial documentation, health insurance setup, pre-arrival paperwork, accommodation booking, banking and SIM arrangements, pre-departure orientation.

02 / Expertise

Each country has own rules.

UK CAS + IHS + ATAS; US SEVIS + DS-160 + visa interview rehearsal; Canada SDS + GIC + biometrics; Australia GTE + OSHC; Malaysia EMGS; Singapore SOLAR + Tuition Grant.

03 / Interview prep

Especially for US F-1.

The US consulate interview is where a lot of strong students stumble. Mock interviews, answer frameworks, document checklists and same-day debriefs.

04 / Pre-departure

Everything you didn't know.

Banking, SIM, health cover, how to open a student account before you arrive, what to pack, what not to pack, first-week logistics.

How it works

Our Visa & Pre-departure process.

CAS / I-20 / CoE review

We verify every detail before you submit anywhere. Small errors here create big problems later.

Financial documentation

Balance statements, sponsor affidavits, GIC arrangements, tax returns — organised to each country's exact standard.

Visa application preparation

Forms filled, evidence bundled, fees tracked, appointments booked (VFS Dubai, US Consulate Dubai, etc.).

Mock interview (if applicable)

US F-1, UK pre-CAS, Canada pre-SDS — realistic rehearsals with common-refusal scenarios.

Pre-departure briefing

Two-hour session with the student + parents: banking, accommodation, health, emergencies, early-week logistics.

Arrival follow-up

We check in during your first week on the ground. Any hiccups, we help.
