Services · Scholarships & Aid

Funding as a design constraint. Not an afterthought.

Scholarship strategy is one of our strongest services. We've secured over ₹4.2 crore in funding for eMBriture families — because we treat funding the way a good architect treats load-bearing walls.

Who it's for

Built for Any applicant who cares about the total-cost number.

This service is valuable for everyone — from families who need aid to cover the gap, to families who don't need it but want to keep their options open. Many scholarships aren't need-based at all.

Our scholarship universe

Hundreds of awards. Curated to your profile.

We maintain a living database of 400+ international scholarships, refreshed every quarter. From the famous (Clarendon, Rhodes, Gates, Chevening, Fulbright, Monbukagakusho) to the obscure (Palestinian Student Bursary at McGill; MacCall MacBain at Cambridge; specific-subject awards at every major university).

Need-blind institutions

The short list worth memorising.

A handful of institutions — MIT, Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Amherst, Bowdoin, Dartmouth, NYU Abu Dhabi — treat international students identically to domestic applicants for aid. If your family qualifies, these can be the cheapest options in the world.

Merit awards

Non-need-based scholarships.

Most universities have automatic entrance scholarships plus named awards with separate applications. We identify every one you qualify for and map the essays so you can stack multiple.

Stacking & negotiation

Yes, you can negotiate.

US private universities, in particular, routinely adjust aid packages when presented with competing offers. We draft and send the negotiation emails.

How it works

Our Scholarships & Aid process.

Scholarship-first shortlist review

Before finalising university shortlist, we map which offer what aid to your profile. Sometimes this changes the shortlist.

Eligibility audit

For every major scholarship (Clarendon, Gates, Chevening, NYUAD etc.), we run a detailed eligibility and fit audit.

Essay & application drafting

Scholarship applications are drafted, reviewed and polished through our senior editors — same process as degree applications.

Financial documentation

CSS Profile, FAFSA (where applicable), university-specific forms. We coordinate with parents on finances sensitively and privately.

Negotiation & acceptance

Once offers land, we negotiate where possible, stack awards where allowed, and help you accept the best package.
